Projects

Robot Arm

Designed, built, wired, and programmed a Robot Arm, that picks up ping pong balls, pencils, dice, and legos, and pennies and places them in containers- submitted to the 2017 Santa Clara Valley Science Olympiad (placed 4th) and 2017 NorCal State Science Olympiad (placed 5th).

Technical Documentation

Hovercraft

Built a Hovercraft that levitated above a flat surface and moved down the track, as close to a target speed as possible. I built it using poplar wood for the base, 8.4V NiMH Batteries, two motors (one for levitation and one for propulsion), and a potentiometer to control and vary the speed. I submitted this to the 2018 Santa Clara Valley Science Olympiad, receiving a medal for finishing 2nd.

Diagrams

Electric Vehicle

Built an Electric Vehicle, that accelerated to its max speed (of 2 meters/second) and came to a complete stop at a given distance, using a brushless DC motor, a NiMH Battery, an encoder, and an Arduino

Mousetrap Vehicle

Built a Mousetrap Vehicle, that moved forward 3m and then reversed, coming to a stop at a given distance, using a two mousetraps, nylon thread, a dowel, and CD's
