Naval Postgraduate School Internship

The project and app I accomplished through my internship at the Naval Postgraduate School

The Project

Using Raspberry Pi and a DJI Phantom Drone, I created a system that would quickly localize, identify, classify, and plot the damage done on a military-operated airfield. Currently working on an Airfield Damage Assessment project that uses computer vision, UAV technology, and GPS navigation to localize and assess damage done on a damaged military air strip.

The App

I am currently in the process of building an Android app that will provide navigating capabilities to the drone for the project, using DJI API's and Android Studio SDK's. The app will enable to drone to fly to a preset GPS waypoint and will also give the user manual flying capabilities. It will also provide the user with a livestream and positioning data.
