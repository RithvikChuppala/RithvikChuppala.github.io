Senior Tech Assitance Initiative

President/Co-Founder

Senior Tech Assistance Initiative (STAI) is a new community organization that aims to help improve tech literacy among seniors in our community. We create high-quality video tutorials designed to help seniors utilize technology, allowing them to incorporate technology into their daily lives. We go directly to senior centers in the Bay Area to give live seminars on technology usage, and assist seniors with technology. We also help put on events for seniors.

I currently serve as President and Co-Founder of this organization. I have created several videos myself and have worked to increase our community involvement.
